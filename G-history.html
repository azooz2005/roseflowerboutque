The Beginning

apr/2021

My mother’s love for flowers inspired the idea for Rose Flower Bouyiuqe.
She always had a small garden at home where she would care for and arrange flowers.
This sparked a passion for beautiful blooms and the joy they brought.
Watching her nurture her garden and seeing the smiles flowers brought to her face,
I realized there was a unique opportunity to share that happiness with others.


Starting Out

aug/2021

Inspired by my mother’s love for flowers, I opened a small,
economical store offering basic flower arrangements.
The idea was simple – provide fresh, affordable flowers to our community.
The store quickly became a favorite spot for locals looking for thoughtful gifts or to brighten their homes.


Expansion

sep/2022

As the store’s popularity grew,
we moved to a more upscale neighborhood and expanded the business.
We added a larger farm to support the store,
ensuring a constant supply of fresh flowers.
This move allowed us to offer a wider variety of flowers and grow our customer base.


Adding Delivery

jul/2023

To make it easier for our customers to access our products,
we introduced delivery services.
This expansion improved our communication with customers and provided a convenient way for them to get flowers whenever they needed.
It was a significant step forward,
enhancing our service and making us more accessible.

Digital Transformation

oct/2024

Observing the rapid development in technology and the increased demand for online services,
we decided to transform Rose Flower Bouyiuqe into a digital store.
This was the best decision we made – it allowed us to reach a broader audience,
offer a more convenient shopping experience,
and stay relevant in an increasingly digital world.
